Introduction

Welcome to Morse Encryptor App!

Here's what you can do with the app:

~ Display each letter and number's morse codes.

~ Display some typical used morse code abbreviations.

~ Vibrate mobile phone by the morse codes.

~ Enter words and translate into morse codes.

The main menu is where you choose the functionality of the app.

Morse Codes: Display a list of each letter and numbers with their morse codes.

You can search letters or numbers by using the searchbar.

Abbreviations: Display a list of typical used morse codes abbreviations.

You can search by their descriptions like "Good Morning" or "Help".

Mobile Vibrate: Select the morse codes and it will vibrate your mobile phone by the tempo of the morse codes.

Encryption: Input words and it will translate into morse codes for you.

The app will also save your last input.
